The Work of Robbie F+*#ing Williams

We've established his musical prowess, but what are the must-hear essentials? Let's take a look at his top three albums.

Escapology

His fifth studio album has wall-to-wall bangers, you can't move for the bangers. It debuted at number one and the song 'Feel', was a worldwide hit. It also featured the first three songs written by Williams alone.

I've Been Expecting You

His second studio album got him his first UK number one. And guess what? More bangers, that's what. It debuted at number one in the UK, and featured 'Millenium' and 'She's the one', both of which absolutely slap.

Sing When You're Winning

The third studio album was just ridiculous. Who doesn't love a bit of 'Rock DJ'? or 'Kids' with Kylie Minogue? No one, that's who. Top tens and number ones across Europe once again cemented his place as a top guy.
